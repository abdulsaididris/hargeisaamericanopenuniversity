Academic Programs

AOU offers a comprehensive range of Islamic Studies programs at the bachelor, master, and doctoral levels.

Bachelor in Islamic Studies

A comprehensive undergraduate program covering Fiqh, Tafseer, Arabic, and Prophetic Biography. 120 credits over 4 years.

Bachelor in Islamic Psychology

Specializations in Islamic Mental Health, Child Psychology and Family Counseling. 123 credits including internship and thesis.

Arabic Language Program

Designed for non-Arabic speakers seeking fluency. Structured levels from beginner to advanced, available to all age groups.

Nobel Qur'an Programs

Youth Qari & Hifz (ages 13–18), Beginners Hifz, and Advanced Qur'anic Performance at the Master & Doctorate levels.

Dual Enrollment

Eligible high school students may enroll in AOU college-level courses for early credit. Contact the admissions office for eligibility.

In-Classroom Learning

All Hargeisa Branch courses are delivered face-to-face in our Hargeisa facilities, led by qualified instructors in Arabic.

About AOU

The American Open University (AOU) was established in 1995 with a mission to provide authentic Islamic education to Muslims worldwide. AOU is a fully accredited institution headquartered in Alexandria, Virginia, USA.

The Hargeisa Branch brings this world-class education directly to the community of Somaliland, offering in-classroom degree programs taught entirely in Arabic.

Our Mission

To educate Muslims with authentic, scholarly Islamic knowledge that promotes personal, family, and community excellence in service of Allah.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of Islamic scholarship and education that produces graduates who are academically excellent, morally upright, and community-focused.
